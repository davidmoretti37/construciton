## App Description (4000 characters max)

```
Construction Manager is the all-in-one app that helps contractors, subcontractors, and construction business owners manage their projects efficiently.

KEY FEATURES:

AI-Powered Estimates
Create accurate project estimates in seconds using AI. Simply describe your project and get instant pricing based on your rates and local market data. Voice-to-text support lets you create estimates hands-free on the job site.

Project Management
Track multiple projects from start to completion with phase-based organization. Monitor progress, manage budgets, and keep everything organized in one place. Real-time dashboards show you exactly where every project stands.

Worker Management
Schedule your crew across multiple projects with our intuitive calendar. Workers can clock in and out with GPS verification, submit daily reports with photos, and view their assigned tasks. Perfect for managing both employees and subcontractors.

Time Tracking
Accurate time tracking with location verification. Workers clock in at job sites, and you get precise records for payroll and billing. Supports hourly, daily, and project-based pay structures.

Financial Tracking
Record expenses and income for each project. See your profit margins in real-time and never lose track of a receipt again. Generate financial reports with one tap.

Professional Invoicing
Create and send professional invoices directly from the app. Track payments, send reminders, and get paid faster. Integrates with your project data for accurate billing.

Client Communication
Keep clients informed with built-in SMS and WhatsApp messaging. Share project updates, send estimates for approval, and maintain professional communication history.

Daily Reports
Workers submit daily reports with photos documenting progress. Owners get a complete record of work performed, perfect for client updates and dispute resolution.

Multi-Language Support
Built for diverse construction crews with support for English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Each user can set their preferred language.

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS:

Starter ($49/month)
- Up to 3 active projects
- Basic AI estimates
- Time tracking
- Invoicing

Pro ($79/month)
- Up to 10 active projects
- Advanced AI features
- Priority support
- All Starter features

Business ($149/month)
- Unlimited projects
- Full AI capabilities
- Dedicated support
- All Pro features

Start with a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime.

Built by contractors, for contractors. Construction Manager understands the unique challenges of running a construction business and gives you the tools to work smarter, not harder.

Download now and take control of your construction business.
```
